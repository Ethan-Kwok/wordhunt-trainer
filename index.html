Board quality is a measure of how "good" a board is based on the total possible amount of points from the board. Data taken from 400 real Word Hunt games.

Any: no minimum for the total possible points.
Average: total possible points must be at or above the mean.
Good: total possible points must be >1 standard deviations above the mean.
Great: total possible points must be >2 standard deviations above the mean.
Amazing: total possible points must be >3 standard deviations above the mean.
